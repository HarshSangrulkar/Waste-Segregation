Waste-Classification


Less developed countries are facing a surge in waste due to urban industrialization. Current waste management, involving burning waste, causes severe air pollution and health risks. Manual waste sorting is hazardous. To protect the environment and health, we need an automated waste separation system based on Convolutional Neural Networks. This system will reduce health risks, improve recycling, and convert waste to energy for economic growth.

Use of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) for waste separation ensures precision and efficiency, reducing health risks for workers. It promotes recycling, protects the environment, and contributes to economic growth, making it a holistic and innovative approach to waste management.
